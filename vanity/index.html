Zetacoin Vanity Address


Everybody in Bitcoin community know Vanity Address.

They are a type of address that start with a nice Prefix that the user select,
like ZZookx3mroB1tsWaf2QGkQbt3zPJgf72XL

thezero@thezero-R580 ~ $ ./vanitygen -Z ZZoo
Difficulty: 78508
Pattern: ZZoo
Address: ZZookx3mroB1tsWaf2QGkQbt3zPJgf72XL
Privkey: 8XQhnxRq7uQEEKBgWJMqpgd6AP811PiypFPHA7spSEzsrD9jfwt


Good, how can I do this?
It's very simple, you have to download vanitygen from zbad405.
This is a Fork of the original vanitygen that support Zetacoin Address.

Before, be sure to have the lastest zetacoin-qt! (Greater than > v0.8.99.13)
Once you have downloaded the zip (Green Button) you are ready!

Type in a terminal windows: ./vanitygen.exe -Z Z[Prefix] and after few minutes (depends on your PC) you will have your vanity address
Remember that the first letter must be a "Z" and the second must be Uppercase!
Before try it you have to test if the privatekey and the address are correct.
You can do this with Zetacoin-qt or with zetacoin-address-utils

On Zetacoin-qt, open the Help > Debug Window menu.
In the new window click on the Console tab

Type: validateaddress {your_vanity_address}

If you see: "isvalid" : true your address is valid and you can go after

If your wallet have a passphrase: walletpassphrase {your_passphrase} 1000

Import the privatekey : importprivkey {your_vanity_privkey} [label]

And if your wallet have a passphrase: walletlock

Congratulation! You have your vanity address! (If the address don't appears in the address list, try reopen zetacoin-qt)
PS: Try it with a Faucet before use it for real transaction.
